Types of Renewable Energy

  • Solar Energy – Energy harnessed from the sun's rays.
  • Wind Energy – Energy captured from wind through turbines.
  • Hydropower – Energy generated by the movement of water.
  • Geothermal Energy – Energy derived from the heat within the Earth's crust.
  • Biomass Energy – Energy produced from organic materials like wood, waste, and other plant matter.
